**1st-level abjuration (ritual)**

**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: Self

**Components**: V, S, M (a pearl worth 10 gp, which the spell consumes)

**Duration**: 8 hours

The next attack made against you within the duration has a -10 penalty to hit.
